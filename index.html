My Recipe

  1. 20 grams of sea salt
  2. 200 grams of whole-wheat flour
  3. 800 grams of Turkey flour
  4. 550 grams of highly active sourdough starter
  5. 700 grams of warm water

Mix salt, wheat flour, and Turkey flour in a large mixing bowl. Add the sourdough starter and water to the flour. Turn and mix all ingredients. Roll the mixture out on a floured countertop. Flattened the dough and fold it together to shape of a ball, cover for half hour in a warm place. Remove the dough ball from mixing bowl, flatten, form into a ball, and repeat one more time. Let dough sit covered in a warm place for about two to three hours (The dough should double in size.) Take the dough out and place it on floured countertop and form it into two balls, and then put them into two proofing baskets covered. Let sit the two proofing baskets in a warm place for another two to three hours. Preheat your oven with enameled Dutch oven to 450 degrees. Put dough from proofing baskets onto parchment paper and place in Dutch ovens covered for 15 minutes. Remove the Dutch oven cover, and continue baking for another 15 minutes. Remove the Dutch oven from the oven. Place bread on a cooling rack for a few minutes. Enjoy!
